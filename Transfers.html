Transfers

Manchester City and Chelsea eyeing Alderweireld


Toby Alderweireld's future at Tottenham is stil in doubt as Chelsea and Man City are targetting him. His deal goes until 2019 but his agent has said that either he gets a new contract from his current side or he receives a transfer offer.If the option to add a further year is taken up,it will mean a $25 million release clause.The Telegraph adds that if Tottenham cannot agree to a new contract, they will probably have to start considering some offers.

Man Utd join race for Lemar:

Man Utd are also targetting Thomas Lemar. The Mirror reports that they will table a club-record deal in January.

Liverpool set $200 million price tag on Coutinho:


Liverpool have refuted Barcelona's claims that they have set a price of €200 million for Philippe Coutinho.

Wenger says he will be back for Monaco's Lemar:


Arsene Wenger has confirmed Arsenal bid €100 million for Monaco midfielder Thomas Lemar on deadline day and says the club will try to sign the France international again.

Leicster City targeting Bacary Sagna:


Leicster City is considering a move for Man City's Sagna. The 34-year-old has made 85 appearances in all competitions for Man City. Sagna is available to join any club now since he is declared a free agent.
